MADDOX didn't get bitten by a radioactive microfiber towel. He didn't fall into a vat of paint sealant. He just cared — cared about every door jamb, every wheel well, every cup-holder crumb, and every inch of leather other shops skipped over.

Born and raised in Gallatin, TN, Maddox grew up tinkering on family cars long before he could legally drive one. What started as Saturday wash sessions in the driveway turned into a full-on obsession with making vehicles look better than the day they rolled off the lot.

QUESTIONS?

F.A.Q. ?

Do you come to me, or do I bring my car to you?

We're a mobile detailing service — we come to you. Driveway, office parking lot, jobsite, or apartment complex anywhere in Gallatin or Hendersonville. We bring our own water and power on the truck, so all you need to do is point us at the car.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Gallatin, Hendersonville, Goodlettsville, Castalian Springs, Bethpage, and Madison — covering most of Sumner County, TN and the north side of Davidson County. If you're outside that radius, call or text us anyway and we'll see what we can do.

How long does a full detail take?

A Sidekick (basic) takes about 1–1.5 hours. The Hero package runs 3–4 hours. The Legend package is 5–7 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. We'll give you an honest time estimate when you book.

Can I text instead of calling?

Yes — text or call (615) 670-3379. Texts often get a faster response since we're frequently elbow-deep in someone else's vehicle.

Do you do trucks, SUVs, and oversized vehicles?

Yes. Trucks, SUVs, vans, and oversized vehicles are priced on inspection — they take longer than a sedan, so the price reflects that. Send a photo when you call or text and we'll quote you on the spot.

How often should I get my car detailed?

For most daily drivers, a full detail twice a year (spring + fall) keeps the paint protected and the interior in great shape. If you commute long distances, have kids, or own pets, every 3–4 months is the sweet spot.

Are quotes free?

Always. Call or text (615) 670-3379, send a photo of your vehicle and a quick description of what you want done, and we'll give you an honest quote — no pressure, no upsells.
